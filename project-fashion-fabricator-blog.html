Fashion Fabricator

Written by

Carissa O'Connell

July 2023; Updated: Jan 2024

Helping to develop a website and app for the Fashion Fabricator has shown me how important it is to understand layouts and UI/UX design. The objective of this app was to allow users to simply custom sewing patterns based on their own measurements. The app would take in the user's measurements and then calcuate the appropriate size each dimension should either be increased or decreased by. The program is originally designed to create sewing patterns for very simple timeless garments meant to be used for many seasons.
